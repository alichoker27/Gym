Our Special Departments

Cardiovascular Department

The Cardiovascular Department is a section in our gym that focuses on exercises that help promote cardiovascular health. It includes a variety of cardio machines such as treadmills, stationary bikes, ellipticals and rowing machines.

Trained staff members are always present

Strength Training Department

The strength-training department has a variety of weights and resistance machines designed to help build muscle and improve strength. Both free weights and machines are available in this department, and it is generally a popular area of the gym.

the gym is staffed by knowledgeable trainers

Group Exercise Department

The classes offered in the Group Exercise Department can vary, but commonly include yoga, Pilates, Zumba, HIIT, dance, and spinning. Most classes are designed to be high-energy, incorporating cardio, strength and flexibility training elements.

The gym also offers a range of wellness programs
